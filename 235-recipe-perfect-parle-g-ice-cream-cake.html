Ingredients of Parle-g ice cream cake

  2. It's 100 g of fresh cream.

  4. It's 1 of mango thinly sliced.
  5. You need 3 tbsp of melted butter.

Parle-g ice cream cake instructions

  1. Take the Parle-G biscuits in a packet and crumble them. Then add the melted butter and mix it properly..
  2. Take the chilled cream in a bowl and beat it with the help of a whisker or an electric beater for 2 to 3 mins..
  3. Add the condensed milk, if you don't have condensed milk you can surely add sugar instead of condensed milk. Beat again till soft peaks form..
  4. Take a round cake mould and add half of the biscuit crumbs into it. Spread well with the help of a spoon..
  5. Now add a layer of the cream and spread evenly then a layer of mango slices..
  6. Repeat the layerings and then refrigerate it overnight..
  7. Garnish it with mango or any other way of your choice..
  8. Your ice cream is ready to serve..
